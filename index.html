What is it ?

GITAM’s annual Homecoming is more than just a reunion; it’s a celebration of our rich heritage and a testament to the enduring bonds forged within our campus walls. This year, we’re excited to welcome our alumni to the beautiful GITAM Bengaluru campus on Saturday, December 21st, 2024.

As we mark a decade since our first graduating class, we invite all GITAM alumni, from every campus and every batch, to come together and relive the magic of their college days.

Join us for a weekend of nostalgia, networking, and fun. Revisit familiar places, reconnect with old friends, and create new memories. Let's celebrate the past and embrace the future together.

GUIDELINES

  • Alumni must register on alumni portal for Homecoming 2024 Bengaluru. On spot Registrations are available at the Bengaluru Campus
  • During prior registration, alumni must specify the required detailes viz. transportation, accompanies, food preferences, parking, accomodation, etc.
  • Alumni should carry the alumni card (if possesed). Alumni must display the registration confirmation mail at the registration desks at the entrance of the campus
  • Attendees must adhere to the strict code of conduct
  • No person who is under influence of alcohol or any other drugs shall not be allowed to enter the campus
  • Attendees are requested to follow the itinerary given in the brochure at the registartion desks
  • Attendees are requested to be well informed about the Homecoming in advance to prevent any assumptions. Alumni can reach out to the mail ID hc2024blr@gitam.edu incase of queries
  • Attendees consent to being recorded, photographed and filmed as part of media coverage of Homecoming. All rights to use, broadcast, stream, sell and promote the media are reserved by the Organizers
  • Attendees are solely responsible for their personal belongings including but not limited to personal vehicles, items, etc.
  • Attendees unconditionally agree to these terms and conditions upon registering for the Homecoming

Frugal Innovation
There is known to be scarcity of certain resources in our country.Frugal innovation can be effective to solve this as it is often driven by a need to do more with less. Innovations in agriculture, education, transportation, water & sanitation, waste management which has potential to scale up the last mile impact. These sectors represent a few areas where frugal innovation is making a significant impact in India. However, frugal innovation can occur across various industries and sectors, driven by the need to create affordable, sustainable, and impactful solutions for the masses.
